Why Software Engineering?


As a lifelong entreprenuer I've always had a passion for being challenged, whether in sports, school or side businesses. This is something that has stuck with me since as long as I can remember.

When I graduated from Endicott I knew I wanted to work for myself. I wanted to work 80 hour weeks, I wanted to have the challenge of knowing I couldn't rely on anyone but myself to be successful and most importantly, I wanted to learn as much as I could.

These last couple of years afforded me the opportunity to 'wear many different hats', which resulted in my introduction to software engineering. Since then, I've known it was something I wanted to do full-time.

I've dedicated the last year to becoming the best engineer possible and I'm looking forward to joining a team where I can learn every day and be a part of something great!
